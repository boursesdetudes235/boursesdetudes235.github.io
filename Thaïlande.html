Thailand International Postgraduate Program (TIPP) 2026

Applications are now open for the Thailand International Postgraduate Program (TIPP) 2026. This is a fully funded scholarship for international students to pursue a Master’s degree at Thai universities. The scholarship is established by the Royal Thai Government and managed by the Thailand International Cooperation Agency (TICA) under the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The TIPP scholarship covers all expenses, and there is no application fee. The program duration ranges from one to two years, offering an excellent opportunity to gain academic experience in Thailand while learning about its rich culture and hospitality.

Program Summary

Host Country: Thailand

Location: Thai Universities

Program: Master’s Degree

Duration: One or Two Years

Benefits: Fully Funded

Deadline: 23rd February 2026

Financial Benefits

The scholarship covers:

  • Return airfare
  • Monthly stipend
  • Visa fees
  • Tuition fees
  • Insurance
  • Airport pickup
  • Settlement, books, and thesis allowance

TIPP 2026 Themes

  • Sufficiency Economy Philosophy (SEP)
  • Public Health
  • Agriculture and Food Security
  • Climate Change and Environmental Issues
  • Bio-Circular-Green Economy (BCG Model)
  • Other topics related to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be nominated by your home country’s National Focal Point
  • Preference for candidates working in government or development-related fields
  • Must hold a Bachelor’s degree in a related field
  • Usually under 45 or 50 years old (depending on the course)
  • Good command of English

Application Process

Applicants can apply online through the official TICA website. Please read the complete application guidelines before submitting your application.
